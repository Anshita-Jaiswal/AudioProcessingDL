## AudioProcessingDL

Introduction to the fundamental signal processing techniques required for Audio Deep Learning.

This notebook covers the essential bridge between raw audio signals and deep learning inputs:

  * Fourier Transforms: Transforming signals from the time domain to the frequency domain.
    
  *  Nyquist Rate & Aliasing: Managing sampling constraints to ensure signal integrity.
    
   * STFT & Spectrograms: Creating time-frequency representations of audio.
    
   * Mel Spectrograms: Rescaling frequency data to match human auditory perception.
    
   * Griffin-Lim: A technique for reconstructing waveforms from magnitude spectrograms.
